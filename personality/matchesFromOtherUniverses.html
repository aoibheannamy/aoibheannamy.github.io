Matching the Corresponding Heroes and Villains to Characters from Film and TV

This is a short study of characters from the Open-Source Psychometrics Project Statistical "Which Character" Personality Quiz. The character's personality data was sourced and put back into the test again to see which characters from other universes they would be most akin to. Finding the closest matches in both protagonists and antagonists provides a good insight to how similar personality types can lead to completely different world views.

https://openpsychometrics.org/tests/characters/
